Notes

  • White fill is deliberate contrast — the bulletin always sits inside a parchment (var(--color-gold-50)) or amber-frame container; the white reads as a crisp printed card rather than part of the wood structure
  • Border uses --color-wood-border (darkest wood) vs --color-wood-dark on parchment panels — the darker tone keeps the thin 2px line legible against white
  • No awning stripe header — white bulletins are flat cards, not notice-board panels; the rivet-only hardware treatment is the sole shared motif
  • Rivets are 5px (vs 7–8px on parchment panels) — the smaller size matches the lighter card scale
  • Item label title uses 'Gaegu' bold, --color-wood-border — handwritten name-tag feel
  • Item label subtitle also uses 'Gaegu' regular, same color, smaller — keeps both lines in the same handwritten register
  • Pig name uses 'Gaegu' bold in --color-wood-mid warm brown — personal name, slightly warmer than the item label dark
  • Breed is 'Nunito' bold, dark neutral --color-neutral-700 — factual label; personality is Nunito regular, same color — less emphasis
  • Gender is 'Nunito' regular, brown --color-wood-dark, align-self:flex-end — sits at the baseline of the flex row
